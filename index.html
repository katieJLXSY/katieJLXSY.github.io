Learn About Rabbits!

Description

Rabbits are small mammals in the family Leporidae. They have short, fluffy tails and long ears. There are 29 species of many different sizes and colors. They live in a variety of enviornments scattered across the world.

Behavior

Rabbits are social animals who live in large groups called colonies. They are most active during dusk and dawn, in which they forage for food, because it gives them the most protection from predators. Their predators include hawks, owls, falcons, wild dogs, feral cats, and ground squirrels.

Rabbit Breeds

There are many different rabbit breeds, some domestic and some wild. Here is a list of domestic rabbits that can be pets.
